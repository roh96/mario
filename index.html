ABOUT

ANIMATION, COMEDY, ADVENTURE, ACTION, FAMILY

Directed by Aaron Horvath and Michael Jelenic (collaborators on Teen Titans Go!, Teen Titans Go! To the Movies) from a screenplay by Matthew Fogel (The LEGO Movie 2: The Second Part, Illumination's Minions: The Rise of Gru),
the film stars Chris Pratt as Mario, Anya Taylor-Joy as Princess Peach, Charlie Day as Luigi, Jack Black as Bowser, Keegan-Michael Key as Toad, Seth Rogen as Donkey Kong, Fred Armisen as Cranky Kong, Kevin Michael Richardson as Kamek and Sebastian Maniscalco as Spike. The film is produced by Illumination founder and CEO Chris Meledandri and by Shigeru Miyamoto for Nintendo.

CHARACTER

  • Mario

    Mario is the protagonist and main character in Nintendo and Illumination Entertainment’s new film, The Super Mario Bros Movie. He is a beloved icon who is known for his trademark red hat adorned with his first initial. Although he is a skilled plumber, Mario’s true calling is being a hero.

  • Princess Peach

    Princess Peach, also known as Princess Peach Toadstool or just Peach, is one of the most recognisable characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. As her title may suggest, she rules over Mushroom Kingdom and lives in Castle Toadstool with her loyal helper, Toad. Although royalty comes with many privileges, Princess Peach often finds herself in trouble because of her position as ruler.

  • Toad

    One of the smallest but most significant characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie is Toad. He is an adorable, cheerful mushroom and loyal friend to Princess Peach. Although tiny, Toad doesn’t let his size stop him. He is a brave character who will stand up for those he cares about and isn’t afraid to fight for what’s right.

  • Bowser

    Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the main antagonist and one of the leading characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. He is a giant anthropomorphic turtle with sharp teeth and a spiked shell. Bowser also has extremely sharp claws and breathes fire. In other words, you don’t want to mess with him. He is the power-hungry ruler of an army of loyal henchmen, including Goombas and Koopa Troopas, who he uses to help him achieve his goal of taking over Mushroom Kingdom.
