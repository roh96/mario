CHARACTER

Mario

Mario is the protagonist and main character in Nintendo and Illumination Entertainment’s new film, The Super Mario Bros Movie. He is a beloved icon who is known for his trademark red hat adorned with his first initial. Although he is a skilled plumber, Mario’s true calling is being a hero. He is always ready for adventure and travels across Mushroom Kingdom time after time to save his friends from danger. In The Super Mario Bros. Movie, Mario is shown to be brave and determined as he embarks on a perilous journey across Mushroom Kingdom with Princess Peach to save the world from evil Bowser.

Princess Peach

Princess Peach, also known as Princess Peach Toadstool or just Peach, is one of the most recognisable characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. As her title may suggest, she rules over Mushroom Kingdom and lives in Castle Toadstool with her loyal helper, Toad. Although royalty comes with many privileges, Princess Peach often finds herself in trouble because of her position as ruler. She is often kidnapped by Bowser, the evil antagonist who wants to take over her kingdom. This time though, it is Luigi who has been kidnapped and Peach joins Mario and Toad on their quest to stop Bowser and save the world.

  • Toad
    One of the smallest but most significant characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie is Toad. He is an adorable, cheerful mushroom and loyal friend to Princess Peach. Although tiny, Toad doesn’t let his size stop him. He is a brave character who will stand up for those he cares about and isn’t afraid to fight for what’s right. He plays a main part in The Super Mario Bros. Movie as he teams up with Mario and Peach to save Mushroom Kingdom from Bowser’s control.
  • Bowser
    Bowser, also known as King Koopa, is the main antagonist and one of the leading characters in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. He is a giant anthropomorphic turtle with sharp teeth and a spiked shell. Bowser also has extremely sharp claws and breathes fire. In other words, you don’t want to mess with him. He is the power-hungry ruler of an army of loyal henchmen, including Goombas and Koopa Troopas, who he uses to help him achieve his goal of taking over Mushroom Kingdom. He is also the father of seven Koopalings. In The Super Mario Bros. Movie, Bowser is yet again trying to take over the world and make Princess Peach his queen using the Power Star. Otherwise, he threatens to use the Star to destroy her kingdom.
  • Luigi
    Voiced by Charlie Day, Luigi plays a large role in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. He is the younger brother of Mario and is known for his iconic green hat. In the film, he is also seen wearing a matching green collared shirt beneath his signature denim overalls. Like his brother, Luigi grew up in Mushroom Kingdom and became a plumber. Although shown to be more cowardly than Mario, he fights evil alongside his brother being brave when it counts and is incredibly loyal to his friends. In The Super Mario Bros. Movie, we see Luigi get separated from his brother as he is kidnapped by Bowser. Will Mario and Princess Peach be able to save him?
  • Donkey Kong
    Donkey Kong (DK) is a semi-friendly, giant gorilla who is a key character in The Super Mario Bros. Movie. He is the Prince of Jungle Kingdom where he lives alongside other members of his species. Unsurprisingly, he loves bananas and you might find an array of slippery skins lying around his kingdom. The popularity of Donkey Kong has also led to the creation of a whole family of new characters including his nephew, Diddy Kong, and his arch-nemesis, King K. Rool. Although DK has been Mario’s main antagonist in Super Mario Bros. video games in the past, he is now a friendly rival of the plumber.
